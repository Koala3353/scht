# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Scht is for individual students balancing classes, deadlines, coursework, personal commitments, and part-time work. They often arrive between classes or during a focused study session, needing to quickly understand what matters next and regain control of a busy week.

## Product Purpose

Scht brings academic planning, grades, course material, personal tasks, integrations, and reminders into one private workspace. It succeeds when students can move from a scattered set of deadlines to an actionable plan without losing the context behind the work.

## Brand Personality

Calm, scholarly, assured. The public experience should feel like a well-kept academic studio: focused, intelligent, and quietly premium rather than loud or gamified.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic productivity-SaaS templates, loud gamified student dashboards, beige "study aesthetic" pages, repeated icon-card grids, and editorial-magazine imitation.

## Design Principles

1. Make a busy academic life feel legible before making it feel feature-rich.
2. Show connected workflows in context, not as a list of integrations.
3. Use precision and restraint to earn trust around personal academic data.
4. Make the next useful action obvious on every screen size.
5. Treat accessibility as academic clarity: readable type, reliable contrast, and motion that never obstructs.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and keyboard access. Respect reduced-motion preferences, preserve readable line lengths, and ensure the design works across mobile, tablet, and desktop study contexts.
